# title 创作快速入门的方法

# chinese 
快速入门的秘密就是：创作最容易上手、最快上手的创作，就是写教程。写任何事情的教程。
# english
The secret to getting started quickly is to engage in the easiest and fastest form of creation: writing tutorials. Write tutorials about anything and everything.
